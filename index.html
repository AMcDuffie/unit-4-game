Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of the crsystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
